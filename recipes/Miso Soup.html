Miso Soup

Description

This is a delicious dish from Japan that you will love!

Ingrediants

  • Dashi (Japanese Soup Stock)
  • MIso Paste
  • Tofu

    • Steps

    1. Gather all ingrediants
    2. Cut 1 green onion
    3. Add 4 cups of water and 1 piece of kombu into a sauce pan
    4. Slowly bring the bowl to a boil for 10 minutes
    5. Add the dashi into the sauce pan
    6. Add 4-5 tablespoons of miso paste
    7. Cut the tofu into cubes and add them to the miso soup
    8. Add 1 tablespoon of seaweed into the pot
    9. Serve and enjoy!
